The og:image must be at least 50px by 50px and have a maximum aspect ratio of 3:1. FB supports PNG, JPEG and GIF formats. You may include multiple og:image tags to associate multiple images with your page. The following tag assumes that your image is already 50x50 as this is a special image with it's own field.
